Main Hall / Headquarters

Freemasons’ Hall, Nairobi

Address: Nyerere Avenue/Road, opposite Serena Hotel, Nairobi

This is the central meeting place for most lodges in Kenya including Lodge Harmony, Victoria Nyanza Lodge, Orient Lodge, Progress Lodge.

Other Kenyan Towns with Lodges

Nairobi: Multiple lodges at Freemasons’ Hall - Lodge Harmony 3084, Victoria Nyanza 3492, Orient 3703

Mombasa: Mombasa Lodge 3645 at FM Hall

Nakuru: Lodge Menengai 3559 at FM Hall

Kisumu: Equator Lodge 4337, Lake Lodge 5022 - founded in 1928

Other towns: Nyeri, Ruiru, Kericho, Kitale
