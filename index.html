The Foundation of Our Brotherhood

Our tradition is rooted in centuries of wisdom, discipline, and unity. In 1717, four London lodges united to form the first Grand Lodge, establishing a formal path for men of character to walk together.

From the craft of builders, we inherited symbols of structure and strength. Today, Freemasonry Heritage Fraternity stands as a brotherhood dedicated to moral growth, mutual support, and service to humanity.
