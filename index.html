The Foundation of Our Brotherhood

Our tradition is rooted in centuries of wisdom, discipline, and unity. In 1717, four London lodges united to form the first Grand Lodge, establishing a formal path for men of character to walk together.

From the craft of builders, we inherited symbols of structure and strength. Today, Freemasonry Heritage Fraternity stands as a brotherhood dedicated to moral growth, mutual support, and service to humanity.

The Pillars That Built Us

Unity in Purpose

When four lodges came together, they created more than an organization. They created a lifelong bond. We continue that tradition today by standing together as one family.

Leadership & Vision

Our early leaders built a structure based on integrity, education, and respect. Their vision guides us to raise men who lead with honor in every area of life.

Timeless Principles

We use the tools of the builder as symbols for building character. Discipline, truth, and brotherly love remain the foundation of every member.

19th Century

Freemasonry Heritage in Kenya

In Kenya, our fraternity was built by men who believed in progress, integrity, and community. From Nairobi to Mombasa, our lodges have become homes of brotherhood where members support one another in business, family, and life.

Here, you are not joining an organization. You are joining a family that will stand with you.
