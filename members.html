Community Projects

Lodge Harmony Meeting

Members gather at Freemasons’ Hall in Nairobi for the monthly stated communication. Brothers in formal regalia discuss lodge affairs, charity projects, and welcome new initiates into the brotherhood. This is where decisions are made to guide our fraternity forward.

Official Meeting

Community Service Initiative

Brothers from Lake Lodge 5022 in Kisumu participate in a community outreach program. Members are seen distributing food and school supplies to local families. This reflects our core value: charity. We believe in giving back to the communities that support us.

Initiation Ceremony

A solemn initiation ceremony taking place at the lodge. Senior members in aprons guide a new candidate through the ancient rites of Freemasonry. This sacred ritual symbolizes enlightenment, moral growth, and the beginning of a lifelong journey of self-improvement.

Business Grant Award

A member receives startup capital through our Life-Changing Business Grant. The Lodge Director presents the check as other brothers look on in support. This program empowers members economically and helps build stronger families and communities.

Brotherhood Fellowship

Members enjoy a fellowship dinner after lodge proceedings. This is where bonds are strengthened outside of formal meetings. Laughter, mentorship, and brotherly love are shared over a meal, reinforcing the unity that defines Freemasonry Heritage.

Mentorship & Learning

Senior brothers mentor new members on the principles of integrity, leadership, and personal development. In this session, experienced members share business knowledge and life lessons to help younger brothers grow in character and success.

Lodge Building Project

Members come together for a community building project. Brothers volunteer their time and resources to renovate the local lodge hall. This demonstrates teamwork, dedication, and pride in maintaining a sacred space for future generations.
