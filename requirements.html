Begin Your Journey With Us

Joining Freemasonry Heritage Fraternity is more than membership. It is a sacred path of brotherhood, honor, growth, and purpose. Here, you will find men of integrity who uplift one another and serve with pride.

Membership Requirements

Your initiation is a process of transformation. You may complete each step in stages as guided by your Lodge Director.

Important Note

It is not a must to pay all requirements at once. The fraternity initiation is a process done in steps. Once you register, your Lodge Director will guide you to the next stage. Each process is conducted on different days of the week to give you time to prepare. We walk with you every step of the way.
